About this map

Pinas 2026 replaces place labels with the stories that made them matter. Each glowing marker is a verified news event from 2026 — tap one to read a summary and jump to the original reporting.

Verification

Every story was checked against the reputable outlets linked inside it (Philippine dailies, wire services, official bodies like the ICC and PHIVOLCS-adjacent monitors, and press-freedom organizations). Nothing on this map is unsourced; dates reflect the events as reported. Data snapshot: July 22, 2026.

Photos

Marker photos are representative images of the place or subject, loaded live from Wikipedia/Wikimedia. For news photography, follow the source links to the original articles.
